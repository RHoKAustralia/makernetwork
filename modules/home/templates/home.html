REX HAZARD

Rex Hazard is a buccaneering pioneer of setting up and working with communities of creative builders, Hacker spaces, co-working spaces and Makers Spaces across the globe. FOOTSCRAY MAKER LAB

Footscray Maker Lab is a creative workspace located at 210 Whitehall St, Footscray. We provide a place for creative people, artists and designers to come together to share knowledge, collaborate and co-create in a variety of projects and initiatives.
